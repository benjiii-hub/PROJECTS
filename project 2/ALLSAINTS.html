About AllSaints Hospital

AllSaints Hospital is a leading healthcare provider in Kampala, Uganda, offering quality medical services ranging from emergency care, maternity, surgery, to community health programs. Our mission is to provide compassionate, affordable, and modern healthcare services to all.
